# HelixLens R&D Platform

## Institutional Overview
HelixLens is a precision genomic engineering console designed for advanced molecular research and clinical variant interpretation. The platform integrates high-fidelity 3D modeling, Bayesian risk assessment, and deterministic CRISPR cleavage simulations to provide a comprehensive R&D environment for genomic scientists.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Genomic Cataloging
Access a curated registry of pathogenic variants and clinical targets. Real-time sequence mapping and primary domain analysis allow for rapid identification of therapeutic loci.

### 2. Clinical Risk Dashboard
Utilizes an integrated Bayesian engine to calculate aggregated risk scores. Modeling considers family pedigree, inheritance patterns, and variant penetrance to predict health outcomes and identify carrier status.

### 3. CRISPR Lab (In Silico)
Standard-v4.2 molecular scanner for simulating Cas9-mediated interventions. 
- **gRNA Design**: Automated motif search for optimal targeting.
- **Repair Simulation**: Models NHEJ and HDR pathways based on local chromatin architecture.
- **Efficiency Scoring**: Real-time prediction of knockout rates and functional impact.

### 4. Scientific Documentation
Generates research-grade reports detailing simulated repair pathways, protein impacts, and clinical recommendations.
